The Issues

What I'm Running On

Chester deserves a Town Board member with a clear, principled agenda — and the skills to execute it.

Fiscal Transparency & Taxes

Residents deserve to understand how every tax dollar is spent — I'll fight for plain-language budgets, open hearings, and zero-based budget reviews.

School District Coordination

Chester's children attend three different school districts — the Town Board should actively coordinate with all three on development, infrastructure, and planning.

Zoning & Smart Growth

Growth should serve residents, not developers — I'll advocate for affordable housing requirements, meaningful community input, and protection of Chester's open land.

Environment & Parks

Chester's green spaces are infrastructure — I'll fight for dedicated parks funding, tree canopy protection, and sustainable development standards.

Arts & Community

A vibrant arts community makes Chester worth living in — I'll ensure arts and culture have a seat at the budget table.
